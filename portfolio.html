ENPH 353 Robot Summer

Over the course of the summer of 2022, my team of 4 students created a robot for the notorious ENPH 353 'robot' summer course. The robot was designed to complete a series of tasks like picking up plastic 'idols' while avoiding obstacles, all while competing against other teams in the class.

Key Achievements:

  • One of only 2 teams to achieve maximum points
  • Developed innovative navigation using IMU and dead reckoning
  • Successfully avoided conventional line-following approach
